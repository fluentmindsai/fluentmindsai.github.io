Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our platform

How quickly can I get started?

You can get started in minutes! Simply sign up for an account, and you'll have immediate access to all the features in your chosen plan. Our onboarding process is designed to get you up and running quickly.

Can I change my plan later?

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing adjustments on your next invoice.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take security very seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, we maintain SOC 2 compliance, and our infrastructure is regularly audited by third-party security firms.

Do you offer customer support?

We offer comprehensive support across all plans. Starter plans include email support, Professional plans get priority support, and Enterprise customers have access to 24/7 phone support and a dedicated account manager.

Can I integrate with other tools?

Yes! We offer integrations with popular tools like Slack, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Salesforce, and many more. Professional and Enterprise plans also include API access for custom integrations.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all plans. No credit card required to start your trial, and you'll have access to all the features of your chosen plan during the trial period.
